ABOUT NUA ODISHA

Welcome to the world of holistic education at NUA ODISHA Skill centwe. At Nua ODisha we believe, that learning is multi-dimensional as we prepare students for the world & challenges for the future.

Nua-Odisha's pedagogical ideology transcends the confines of the classrooms & pages of a textbook to a more multi-faceted approach. In keeping with the theory of "Multiple Intelligence," we believe that every child has some latent potential.

Apart from the prescribed academic curriculum, our infrastructure is designed to provide opportunities for students to develop their full potential.

FOUNDERS DAY 13/04/2024

As we celebrate our history, we humbly honour our past & pledge to work towards a brighter future. We are the front runners in bringing the concept of holistic education to Odisha & sharpening the minds of so many children since 2003. Our alumni has shining examples in the field of Engineering, Medicine, Liberal Arts, Sports, Management, Law, Business & many more. The future of India is in our classrooms today being sculpted to face a brighter tomorrow. Congratulations to the DPS Kalinga family @ 21 ! We are thankful to the Almighty for this great milestone & pray for prosperity & success in the times to come as we enrich the lives of so many children everyday. Bande Utkal Janani.
